Frequently Asked Questions

Is this site showing live data?

No, this is an educational project with manually curated data for demonstration purposes.

Can I subscribe to real match alerts?

The subscription feature is a demo and does not send actual emails.

Where does the data come from?

Data is sourced from official league websites (UEFA, LaLiga, The FA, Liga MX, NWSL) and ESPN.

How can I report an error?

Please use the contact form and select "Report a Bug" from the subject dropdown.
